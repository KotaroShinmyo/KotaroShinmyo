About Me

Broadly speaking, I am interested in quantum gravity and quantum field theory. In particular, I am fascinated by how our universe may be realized from a theory of quantum gravity such as string theory. I am also interested in studying non-perturbative effects in strongly coupled quantum field theories. One powerful principle expected in quantum gravity is the holographic principle, and I use holography to explore properties of quantum gravity and quantum field theory. Nice to meet you, and thanks for visiting.
